Detailed Description

The lowest level base class of an IGraphics control.

A control is anything on the GUI, it could be a static bitmap, or something that moves or changes. The control could manipulate bitmaps or do run-time vector drawing, or whatever. Some controls respond to mouse actions, either by moving a bitmap, transforming a bitmap, or cycling through a set of bitmaps. Other controls are readouts only.
